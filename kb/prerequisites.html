Prerequisites for a Home Server

Last updated: Feb 10, 2026

Before setup, make sure your home and hardware are ready for an always-on server.

Computer requirements

  • A computer that can install Docker Desktop (Windows 10/11 or macOS).
  • Enough storage for your library and future growth.
  • Reliable power and ventilation.

Network requirements

  • Fast home internet for smooth access and streaming.
  • A wired LAN connection is preferred for stability.
  • Place the server close to your router if possible.

Placement & uptime

  • Plan for the computer to stay on all the time.
  • Store it in a safe, dry spot in your home.
  • Keep it on a solid surface with airflow.
